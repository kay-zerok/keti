Frequently Asked Questions

We offer a 30-day return policy on all unworn, unwashed items with original tags attached. Simply bring your receipt and the items to any of our stores or follow our online return process.

Currently, we only ship within South Africa. However, we're working on expanding our shipping options to include international destinations in the near future.

Once your order has been shipped, you'll receive a tracking number via email. You can use this number on our website's tracking page or with the courier's website to monitor your package's progress.

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), PayPal, and EFT payments. In-store, we also accept cash and mobile payments like SnapScan.
