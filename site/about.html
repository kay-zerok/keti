Our Story

Discover the passion and craftsmanship behind Keti.

Redefining Fashion Since 2008

Keti was born from a simple idea: to create high-quality, stylish apparel that stands the test of time. What started as a small boutique in Pretoria has grown into a beloved brand across South Africa, known for our commitment to quality, sustainability, and timeless design.

Our Craftsmanship

Every Keti garment is crafted with meticulous attention to detail. Our team of skilled artisans combines traditional techniques with modern innovation to create pieces that are both beautiful and durable.

We source only the finest materials, ensuring each piece feels as good as it looks. From the stitching to the finishing touches, quality is never compromised.

Sustainable Practices

At Keti, we believe fashion should be both stylish and sustainable. We're committed to reducing our environmental impact through responsible sourcing, ethical production, and long-lasting designs.

Our sustainability initiatives include using organic cotton, reducing water consumption in production, and implementing a recycling program for old garments.

Our Values

Quality

We never compromise on quality. Every stitch, every fabric, every detail is carefully considered to ensure excellence.

Sustainability

We're committed to ethical practices that respect both people and the planet throughout our supply chain.

Community

We believe in giving back and supporting the communities that make our business possible.
